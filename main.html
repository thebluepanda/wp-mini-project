Maharashtra is a state in the western region of India and is India's second-most populous state and third-largest state by area. It is also the world's second-most populous sub-national entity. It has over 112 million inhabitants and its capital, Mumbai, has a population of approximately 18 million. Nagpur is Maharashtra's second capital as well as its winter capital. Maharashtra's business opportunities along with its potential to offer a higher standard of living attract migrants from all over India.

M-life is a website made for a person to get the feeling like they belong here .
